Translation (Spanish)

"Diseñada para condiciones extremas, esta batería de alta capacidad de 20,000 mAh cuenta con un exterior robusto con certificación IP67 de resistencia al agua y tecnología de carga rápida avanzada, ideal para el viajero moderno. Ya sea que esté explorando los Andes o viajando por la ciudad, manténgase conectado sin limitaciones."

📝 Translator's Notes

This project required "Transcreation"—the process of adapting a message from one language to another while maintaining its intent, style, tone, and context. I avoided literal translations for terms like "engineered" and "without compromise" to ensure the emotional resonance of the brand remained strong in all three target cultures. I also verified that all technical units and punctuation (such as decimal commas in German) were localized for the specific region.
